Timeline

Partly inspired by Chris’ and Zach’s timelines, here’s a list of events that were really important for me:

10/2017
Met Jo.
06/2016
Navigated the American visa process and moved to San Francisco to join the team at Gusto as a product designer and UI engineer.
12/2014
Along with Andy Baio and Andy McMillan, I designed and developed a website for their co-working space in Portland called The Outpost.
12/2014
Joined the writing team at CSS-Tricks and began writing regularly for the website, covering all sorts of front-end development and design topics.
06/2014
I left Erskine and started freelancing as a front-end developer and designer (I moved into my mom’s loft).
11/2012
Moved to Nottingham in the UK to start working with the team at Erskine as a front-end developer. Figured out the command line and CSS.
02/2012
Worked with the writer/editor Alex Charchar on my first piece for Smashing Magazine. It was a long and grueling process because I was learning how to write and design at the same time.
10/2011
Studied an MA in Information Design at the prestigious and scary Department of Typography and Graphic Communication at Reading University. Kinda had my ego thrown into the heart of the nearest star.
10/2008
Started my undergrad degree at Plymouth University in English Literature.
