Timeline

Partly inspired by Chris’ and Zach’s timelines, here’s a list of events that were really important for me:

10/2017
Met Jo.
06/2016
Navigated the American visa process and moved to San Francisco to join the team at Gusto as a product designer and UI engineer.
12/2014
Along with Andy Baio and Andy McMillan, I designed and developed a website for their co-working space in Portland called The Outpost.
12/2014
Joined the writing team at CSS-Tricks and started writing regularly for the website, covering all sorts of front-end development and design topics.
06/2014
I left Erskine and started freelancing as a front-end developer and designer.
11/2012
Moved to Nottingham in the UK to start working with the team at Erskine as a front-end developer.
02/2012
Worked with Alex Charchar on my first piece for Smashing Magazine.
10/2011
Studied an MA in Information Design at the prestigious and scary Department of Typography and Graphic Communication at Reading University.
10/2008
Graduated from Plymouth University with a BA in English Literature.
