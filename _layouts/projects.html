My name’s Robin and I’m a product designer, writer, and developer from the UK.

Hello there! I’m fascinated by design systems, front-end development, UX and visual design. Today I work at Gusto where I work on a combination of projects like this: I develop our pattern library and styleguide, contribute to the foundations of the front-end codebase, and work on the underlying UX of the application including new features and services for Gusto’s customers.

I also write, edit, and publish articles about design and front-end development alongside the team at CSS-Tricks. This is where I learn about how browsers work but it’s also how I keep on top of the latest advances in design and front-end development.
