Newsletter

Adventures in Typography is a newsletter all about, well, typography, yes. But it’s also about publishing and graphic design. It’s about calligraphy and lettering, display type and micro type (and what those words mean). It’s a newsletter about books written about fonts, it’s all about type specimens and neon lights, posters and morse code. Of course that’s without mentioning stamps, illustrations, design systems, the madness of the CSS float property, AI storytelling and books about seeds.
