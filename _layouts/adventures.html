Adventures in Typography

This is my weekly newsletter on typography, graphic design and the web. I share what I’m working on, what I’ve been thinking about and what I’m reading. Here’s my pitch:

Adventures in Typography is a weekly love letter to the written word. Topics include: calligraphy, lettering, display type, micro type, books about fonts, type specimens, neon lights, posters, morse code, stamps, literature, web design, and books about seeds.

If you happen to be interested in typography or design then you can check out an example from earlier this year or follow along.
