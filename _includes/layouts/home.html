Adventures in Typography

My current side project is a newsletter all about typefaces and fonts (and what those words mean) called Adventures in Typography. It’s for book nerds, neon light enthusiasts, and for people who have no interest in typography whatsoever. Each week I write about a stroll through the city and a letter that caught my eye, or a beautiful book, or even a wiggly videogame.
