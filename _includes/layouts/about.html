About me

I’m a web designer from the south west of England and now I live in cloudy San Francisco where I work as a product designer at Gusto. My day job is spent building our design system and contributing to the foundations of our front-end codebase.

I also write, edit, and publish things alongside the team at CSS-Tricks. Although most of my time is now focused on the newsletter where we collect everything that happened in this little community over the course of any given week. So if you’re interested in front-end development then I’d highly recommend you sign up.
